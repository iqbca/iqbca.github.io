ETF / Mutual Fund Rebalancing Optimizer

Problem

Most portfolios are rebalanced using fixed calendar rules, ignoring risk drift, volatility regimes, and turnover constraints.

System Design

  • Drift-based monitoring versus target allocation
  • Risk contribution and volatility-aware triggers
  • Constraint handling (min/max weights, turnover limits)
  • Separation of signal, decision, and execution layers

Outcome

Rebalancing decisions became explainable, auditable, and aligned with portfolio risk rather than calendar timing.
