What I Actually Do

Most analytics problems in investment teams are not caused by lack of intelligence or modelling sophistication. They are caused by fragmented workflows, uncontrolled assumptions, and manual processes that do not scale.

Portfolio Analytics Platforms

Designing portfolio analytics systems delivering performance, attribution, risk metrics, rolling returns, and diagnostics consistently across portfolios and reporting cycles.

Reporting & Workflow Automation

Automating recurring reporting workflows to reduce spreadsheet dependency while improving validation, auditability, and turnaround times.

AI-Assisted Insight Layers

Building AI-assisted systems that convert analytics outputs into structured, explainable commentary — without turning decision-making into a black box.

How I Think About Analytics Systems

Strong analytics is not defined by complexity — it is defined by trust. If a system cannot be explained, validated, repeated, and reviewed, it eventually becomes a liability rather than an asset.

I prioritise clear separation of data, logic, assumptions, and outputs, automation that improves control rather than speed, reduction of key-person risk, and systems that scale across portfolios, managers, and reporting cycles without breaking.
