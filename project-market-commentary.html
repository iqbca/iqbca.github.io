AI Market Outlook Commentary

This case study documents a governed AI-assisted framework designed to generate market outlook commentary aligned with macroeconomic indicators and underlying economic assumptions.

The objective was to standardise market narratives while avoiding opinion drift, inconsistency, or unsupported claims.

Context & Problem

Market outlook commentary is often subjective, style-dependent, and difficult to keep consistent across analysts and reporting cycles.

Manual drafting introduced variation in tone, depth, and emphasis, even when underlying macro views were unchanged.

Design Principles

Assumption-Led

All commentary must trace back to defined economic assumptions.

Macro Anchoring

Narrative generation tied to observable macro indicators and data trends.

Governance

Guardrails to prevent speculative or unsupported statements.

Framework Design

The system combines structured macro inputs, predefined narrative templates, and controlled AI interpretation.

Commentary logic is segmented by asset class and economic theme, ensuring relevance and clarity.

Analysts retain final review and approval responsibility before publication.

Outputs & Coverage

Macro Environment

Inflation trends, growth expectations, and policy context.

Asset-Class Views

Equity, fixed income, and multi-asset outlook summaries.

Risk Considerations

Identification of macro risks influencing portfolio outcomes.

Impact & Outcomes

The framework improved consistency of market outlook narratives, reduced drafting effort, and ensured alignment between commentary and economic assumptions.

It also reduced reputational and interpretative risk associated with ad-hoc market commentary.

Key Takeaways

Market commentary benefits most from structure and constraint. AI is effective when it amplifies disciplined thinking rather than generating opinions.
