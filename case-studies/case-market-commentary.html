AI-Powered Market Outlook Commentary

A structured commentary generation system designed to produce consistent, repeatable market outlook narratives using macro indicators and rule-based logic.

Problem Context

Market outlook commentary is often subjective, inconsistent across analysts, and difficult to reproduce across reporting cycles. Manual drafting also introduces time pressure and stylistic variability.

System Approach

The solution combines predefined macroeconomic indicators, classification rules, and controlled language templates to generate structured commentary aligned with underlying data signals.

Governance & Control

Rather than free-form AI output, the system enforces guardrails to ensure interpretability, consistency, and alignment with approved assumptions.

Outcome

The framework significantly reduces analyst effort while improving consistency and comparability of market outlook narratives across periods.
