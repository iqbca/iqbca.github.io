Automated Financial Modelling Framework

A reusable, parameter-driven modelling framework designed to replace manual spreadsheet-based financial models.

Problem Context

Traditional financial models are often rebuilt from scratch, leading to inconsistent assumptions, structural errors, and limited scalability.

Design Philosophy

The framework separates assumptions, calculations, and outputs, enabling controlled scenario analysis and reducing the risk of accidental overrides.

Automation Layer

Parameterisation and automation allow rapid scenario testing while maintaining consistency across model runs and reporting cycles.

Impact

The system improves modelling speed, consistency, and transparency, making financial analysis easier to review, reuse, and audit.
