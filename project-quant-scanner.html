Quantitative Equity Scanner / Algo Engine

Problem

Many equity scanners operate as opaque black boxes, offering signals without context or validation tooling.

System Design

  • Index-conditioned market logic
  • Layered filters (volatility, distribution, price movement)
  • Normal and rapid-signal modes
  • Real-time scan visibility and debugging output

Outcome

The system prioritizes signal transparency and validation, enabling parameter tuning and logic verification.
